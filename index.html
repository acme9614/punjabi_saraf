The Punjabi Saraf Story

At Punjabi Saraf, we have been proudly crafting exquisite jewellery for generations. Established in 1947 in the vibrant city of Indore, we have earned a reputation as a pioneer in designer jewellery. Honoured with numerous National Awards, our brand is renowned for its blend of traditional craftsmanship and contemporary design.

Our commitment to excellence extends across every piece we create, from intricate bridal jewellery to elegant daily wear. As Indore’s leading jewellery brand, we continue to innovate while staying true to our heritage of unmatched quality and artistry.

Discover the legacy of Punjabi Saraf, where tradition meets modern elegance, making us a trusted name in the world of jewellery.
